You can save search results as well as other Werkstatt pages in your account so that you can call them back up later. To do this you must be logged in. In this case we have searched for "regnum". Here we see the search results. We want to look more closely at the search results for Marculf I,1. Here we see the formula Marculf I,1 and the search result "regnum". We want to save this page for later. So we go to our account and click on "Save this page". We can give this page a name, for instance Marculf I,1. And we want to be sure to save the search results. Then we click on "Save Page". The page is now saved and can be called up again using your account. To do this go to your account and click on "Saved Pages". Here you see all the pages that you have saved. You can delete a page by clicking "Remove from the list". To call a page up again simply click on the page name. And the search results are brought up again with the yellow highlighting.
