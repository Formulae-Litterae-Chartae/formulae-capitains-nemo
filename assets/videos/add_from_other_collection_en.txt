You can add a text from another collection by clicking on "More texts" and following the trail to the text that you want.
In this case, we want to open the formula Tours 44 next to Marculf II,35.
So we click on "Formulae", then "Formelsammlung aus Tours", then "[Edition]" by Tours 44.
Now we can compare the texts of Marculf II,35 with Tours 44.
And, as always, to close one of these texts, simply click on the X symbol in the upper right-hand corner.
