The quickest way to search the Werkstatt is with the simple search in the upper right-hand corner. You can search formulae collections, charter corpora or E-Lexicon articles. There is also a lemma search and an advanced search. These will be covered in their own videos. To search for a term, go to the simple search field, and type in the phrase for which you want to search, suggestions for phrases to search are shown (you need not choose any of these), then click on the box(es) for the corpora you wish to search, Formulae and Charters in this case, and then hit "Enter". The Werkstatt will be searched and the results shown here. You can filter, sort and download these search results. These functions will be explained in their own videos.
